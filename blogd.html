4 tips to keep your plants healthy!

here i am sharing my daily routine of how i take care of my plants and some dos and donts

How to take care of indoor plants?

Well here are some extra tips for you

1.Water plants regularly.
2.Check the soil moisture.
3.Pour water at room temperature.
4.Give some sunlight or artificial light to plants.
5.Use humidifier to maintain surroundings.
6.Add fertilizers to the pot to provide nutrients to plant.
7.Prune your plant regularly.
